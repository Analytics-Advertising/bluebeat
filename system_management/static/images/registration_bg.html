TO OPAL STUDENT ACCOMMODATION

Moving into student accommodation can be exciting but equally as daunting.

There is a lot to consider – from sharing your space with a stranger, adapting to studying and living independently, and managing your time and funds.

WHY OPAL?

Style and comfort don’t have to cost an arm and a leg in 2024

The Opal provides a comfortable and personal living space for UJ Soweto Campus students with all the convenient amenities, at affordable rates. Whether you would like to stay alone or share, there is something for your preference and budget. 

  • Style and comfort, brand new buildings 
  • Interruption free studying  
  • Long-term connections  
  • Balanced study and play life 
  • Hassle-free registration 
  • NSFAS Accredited
